*ABOUT THE DATASET*
Based on information about this dataset downloaded from Kaggle, screening questions from A1 to A9 were:
A1: Does your child look at you when you call his/her name?
A2: How easy it is for you the get eye contact with your child?
A3: Does your child point to indicate that he/she wants something? (eg. a toy that is out of reach)
A4: Does your child point to share interest with you? (eg. pointing at an interesting sight)
A5: Does your child pretend? (eg. talks on a toy phone)
A6: Does your child follow where you are looking?
A7: If you or someone else in the family is visibly upset, does your child show signs of wanting to comfort them?
A8: Would you describe your child's first word as typical or not?
A9: Does your child use simple gestures? (eg. wave goodbye)

Column "Class" shows presence of ASD based on screening score results from questions from A1 to A10 (1 - ASD traits, 0 - no ASD traits)
I did not include question A10 in my analysis due to reverse scoring, which can lead to inadequate results.
Exact info about the questions and possible answers is here on Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/asdpredictioninsaudi/asd-screening-data-for-toddlers-in-saudi-arabia
